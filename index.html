Payment & Submission

Payment Methods & Policies — Please Read Before Paying

  • Direct Deposit: Available for local (Sri Lanka) attendees only.
  • Wire Transfer: Payment must be received within 30 days of registration.
  • Debit/Credit Card: A 1.5% processing commission will be added to your total at checkout.
  • Refund Policy: All refund requests must be sent to sicet@sliit.lk by August 23, 2025. An administrative fee of US$20 / LKR 6,000 applies to all refunds.

Steps to complete your registration:
  ① Select your registration type(s) above and fill in all required details.
  ② Download the Proforma Invoice (appears above once type is selected) to see your total fee.
  ③ Make payment at the SLIIT portal following the instructions below.
  ④ Upload your payment receipt below and click Submit Registration to confirm.

How to Make Your Payment

  1. Visit the SLIIT payment portal: https://pay.sliit.lk/
  2. Under Purpose of Payment, select "Any other payment" from the dropdown.
  3. In the Description field, enter: "SICET 2026"
  4. Complete the payment and save your transaction receipt.
  5. Upload the receipt and enter the Transaction Reference Number below.
After completing payment, download the receipt or take a screenshot and upload it here. Accepted formats: JPEG, PNG, PDF (max 5MB).
