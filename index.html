Payment Methods & Policies

  • Direct Deposit: Available for local (Sri Lanka) attendees only.
  • Wire Transfer: Payment must be received within 30 days of registration.
  • Debit/Credit Card: A 1.5% processing commission will be added to your total at checkout.
  • Refund Policy: All refund requests must be sent to sicet@sliit.lk by August 23, 2025. An administrative fee of US$20 / LKR 6,000 applies to all refunds.

How to complete your registration:
  ① Fill in all details above and download the Proforma Invoice.
  ② Click "Save & Get Reference ID" — your details are saved and a unique Reference ID is issued.
  ③ Make payment using the instructions below. Use your Reference ID as the payment description.
  ④ Upload your receipt in Step 2 below and click Submit Registration.

How to Make Your Payment

  1. Visit the SLIIT payment portal: https://pay.sliit.lk/
  2. Under Purpose of Payment, select "Any other payment".
  3. In the Description field, enter your Reference ID (shown above).
  4. Complete the payment and save your transaction receipt.
  5. Upload the receipt below and click Submit.
